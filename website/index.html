How it works

Three steps to focus

1

Write your task

Add what you want to accomplish. Be specific — "Write intro for pitch deck" works better than "Work on pitch."

2

Lock in

Hold Enter or click Lock In. Your window goes dark, showing only your current task. Aquwity starts watching your screen in the background.

3

Stay on track

If you drift — scrolling Twitter, browsing Reddit — Aquwity notices. The bar warms to red, then locks distracting apps on your phone. Get back on task and everything resets.
